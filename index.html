Our platform

ExpirySmart™ — retail expiry intelligence built for East Africa.

ExpirySmart™ is a product of Circlio Technologies Limited. It gives supermarket operations teams real-time visibility into expiry risk across their stores — and the tools to act on it automatically, intelligently, and profitably.

Expiry Risk Intelligence
Prioritised visibility into which products, in which stores, are approaching expiry — before it becomes a write-off.
Automated Markdowns
Dynamic pricing and discount rules that convert near-expiry risk into recovered revenue rather than waste.
Customer Loyalty Engine
Rewards that bring customers back to buy near-expiry deals — turning waste reduction into a loyalty programme.
Operations Dashboard
A single view for store managers and head office — real data, real decisions, no spreadsheets.
